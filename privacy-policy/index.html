FieldAlytics Privacy Policy – Your Data, Clearly Protected

Last Updated: March 15, 2024

At FieldAlytics, we place paramount importance on protecting your personal data. This privacy policy aims to clearly explain how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our website serving Canadian users. We are committed to complying with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable Canadian privacy legislation.

What Data We Collect and How It Enhances Your Experience

To provide you with the best possible services, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Voluntarily provided personal information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and other information necessary to provide our analytics services.
  • Browsing data: We gather information about your interaction with our site, including your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and visit duration.
  • Location data: With your consent, we may collect information about your location to personalize services offered to Canadian users.
  • Analytics preferences: Your data visualization preferences, dashboard configurations, and report settings.

This data allows us to:

  1. Personalize your user experience according to your professional preferences

  2. Continuously improve our analytics services and features

  3. Provide you with effective and personalized customer support

  4. Ensure the security of your account and prevent unauthorized access

  5. Inform you about relevant updates and features (with your consent)

The Essential Role of Cookies in Personalizing FieldAlytics

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that enhance your experience on our site. We use different types of cookies:

Cookie Type Purpose Retention Period
Essential cookies Basic website functionality (login, preferences) Session or 12 months
Performance cookies Website usage analysis and improvement 24 months
Functionality cookies Remembering your preferences for Canadian users 12 months
Analytics cookies Content optimization and user behavior analysis 12 months

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit some functionalities of our analytics platform.

How We Use Your Data Responsibly to Improve Our Services

We use your data strictly in accordance with the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Contract execution: To provide the analytics services you requested and manage your account.
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our services, prevent unauthorized access, and ensure platform security.
  • Consent: To send you communications about analytics features and updates when you have explicitly agreed to receive them.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable Canadian laws and regulations.

We only process your data for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes for which it was collected, in compliance with Canadian legal requirements.

Strict Protocols for Data Sharing with Trusted Partners

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information with:

  1. Service providers: Partners who help us operate our analytics platform (hosting, analytics, security) under strict confidentiality.

  2. Legal authorities: When required by Canadian law or as part of legal proceedings.

  3. Analytics partners: Only with your explicit prior consent for specific integrations.

All our partners are carefully selected and contractually obligated to protect your data in accordance with Canadian privacy standards. For transfers outside Canada, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Your Rights to Access, Modify, or Delete Your Data at Any Time

In accordance with Canadian privacy laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Obtain confirmation that we are processing your data and access to it.
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to restriction: Request limitation of processing in specific situations.
  • Right to object: Object to processing for legitimate reasons.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise these rights, contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@fieldalytics.ca. We will respond within one month as required by Canadian regulations.

Keeping You Informed – Timely Updates on Privacy Policies

We are committed to informing you of any substantial changes to this privacy policy. Updates will be posted on this page with an indication of the last revision date. For significant changes, we will notify you by email or through a visible notification on our site.

We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
